Ernesto's Cafe

Type

Cafe

Location

New York City

Status

Completed 2021

Area

800 square feet

Scope

Interior architecture FF&E

Ernesto's Cafe is a more casual, modern take on Basque architectural influences for the daytime space accompanying Ernesto's restaurant.

The cafe extends the design language of the adjacent restaurant into a lighter, more approachable setting. Natural materials and carefully considered details create a welcoming atmosphere for daytime dining and coffee service.
