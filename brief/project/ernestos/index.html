Ernesto's is a celebrated Basque-inspired restaurant in New York City's Lower East Side, where art nouveau architecture meets modern dining.

The design channels the organic forms and ornamental richness of Basque art nouveau through a contemporary lens. Custom lighting fixtures, handcrafted tilework, and carefully sourced materials create an atmosphere that is both elevated and welcoming.
