Work With Us

Progress is a mission-driven design studio with a core interest in conscious, ethical design. From designing custom light fixtures & furniture to 200-key hotel properties, no project is too big or too small. Together we can create beautiful spaces and objects with a holistic approach.

  • Commercial interior architecture, including hospitality and retail

  • Residential design, from renovations to new builds, with a focus on healthy materials and sustainable approaches

  • Product design, including furniture, lighting, and home goods

  • Sustainable and Healthy Material consulting

  • Material development, with a focus on regenerative and natural materials

About

Progress is the design studio of Michael Progress. Michael is an award winning designer known for his thoughtful approach to space, blending functionality, narrative, and deep appreciation for craft. His background in architecture and design informs his work, creating environments that speak to both the practical and the poetic aspects of space. Progress's projects, characterized by their attention to detail and user experience, reflect an unpretentious yet impactful vision for contemporary design.

Michael Progress is a distinguished designer with a rich academic and professional background. He holds a Master of Advanced Architecture from the Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalunya, along with a Bachelor of Arts in Architectural Studies from Brown University. His firm, founded in 2013 and based in Barcelona, Spain, specializes in creating unique spaces that emphasize functionality, brand narrative, and customer experience. Progress's work has been recognized in prestigious publications and has earned several awards, including the Hospitality Design Awards. He also contributes to the design community through teaching and serving on boards for nonprofits.
