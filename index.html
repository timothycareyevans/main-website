Frequently Asked Questions

All Services Process
What kinds of projects do you take on?

I take on cybersecurity consulting, AI prototypes, creative web builds, systems design, and focused launch support for startups and ambitious teams.

Can you help with both design and technical execution?

Yes. A lot of the value is in bridging taste, messaging, frontend execution, and technical systems thinking in one lane.

Do you work on short sprints or longer engagements?

Both. Some projects are best handled as fast, clearly scoped sprints, while others benefit from an ongoing advisory or build partnership.

What does your process usually look like?

The usual flow is discovery, direction, execution, and refinement. I try to keep things sharp, practical, and grounded in the real outcome you want.

How do we get started?

Reach out through the contact page or footer form with what you are building, what kind of help you need, and your timeline. From there we can scope the right fit.
